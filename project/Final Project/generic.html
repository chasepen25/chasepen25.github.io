Notes About My Taste

There are a few things I notice a lot in the movies I enjoy most.

I usually pay attention to pacing, music, visuals, and the ending.

  • I like movies with strong visuals.
  • I remember movies more when the ending is good.
  • I rate movies higher when they are fun and well made.
